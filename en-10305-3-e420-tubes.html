EN 10305 E420 Precision Steel Tubes

EN 10305-3 Steel Tubes for Precision Applications Welded Cold Sized Tubes Manufacturers

Amardeep Steel is a major manufacturer of high-quality BS EN 10305-3 E420 steel. EN 10305 and E420 are distinguished by precisely defined dimension tolerances and a specified surface roughness. The furniture, automobile, and general engineering sectors are typical domains of application. The standard BS EN 10305-3 E420 required an outer diameter of 193,7 mm. 12 000 m tube with an outer diameter of 40 mm and a wall thickness of 1,5 mm, produced of pickled, normalized steel , to be delivered in a standard length of 6 m in compliance with EN 10305-3 E420.
